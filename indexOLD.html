About

I'm a creative interaction designer who brings passion and experience to the work environment. My experience includes working for software companies, interactive TV, large media companies, and a news organization. My core design principles keep the user in the fore-front of a project, while maintaining the big picture and end goals.

My professional passions range from UI and UX design and development. I’m passionate about creating products that people like to use. I use design to solve challenges and communicate solutions. I enjoy creating HTML and CSS products that mirror the design.

I believe the way information is presented dictates how a user digests and learns information, and I enjoy being the bridge that brings the information to the user in an appealing, user friendly fashion. I love what I do, and refill with a cup of coffee.

In my spare time I paint oils and enjoy photography.
